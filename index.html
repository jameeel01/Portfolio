About Me

I'm a student developer at the British Columbia Institute of Technology, currently pursuing a diploma in Computer Systems Technology. My journey into tech began when I switched from a Health Science major to Computer Science, thanks to recommendations from friends and former professors.

What started as a career pivot has become a genuine passion. I thoroughly enjoy creating with code and bringing ideas to life through technology. Whether it's building full-stack web applications or solving complex problems, I find myself unlocking new gears in the development process.

Born and raised in beautiful British Columbia, I balance my screentime with outdoor adventures. You'll find me snowboarding in the winter and golfing in the summer. Otherwise I enjoy spending time with my cat, Taro.
